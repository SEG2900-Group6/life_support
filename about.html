Meet the Team Life Support is composed of passionate software engineering students who share a passion for the environment. Through common problems of short battery life, we are united. We deliver clean and bug-free code to reliably charge your devices. Our members are involved in their community, often visible at protests and cleaning up the streets in their spare time. We strive to bring these technologies all around the globe.
Our Vision Life Support helps reducing battery waste and is an advocate for electrification worldwide in order to phase out the use of carbon-emitting fuels. That’s precisely why we offer our “Send your Gizmos” program to recycle these useful minerals that would have gone to waste otherwise. As one of the biggest environmentalists in the tech industry, we believe in the future. . Life Support recognizes that individuals and companies must do their part to contribute towards fighting against climate change. What the planet needs at the moment is Life Support.
