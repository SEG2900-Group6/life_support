How it Works

Life Support Software

The Life Support software implements machine learning to automatically optimize your battery life based on the usage of your device. We offers the option of customizability for those who know what they want.

Through recurrent neural networks, Life Support determines the apps that you don't use and prevents them from using your battery life. We can also pause wifi and data usage to save battery if deemed battery-draining.

Life Support also works while you’re charging. By testing billions of charging scenarios in simulations, our software found the optimal rate of charging for all existing devices and charges based on that.

Life Support Charger

The hardware is simple, it comes with all of the software but optimized to work 50% better. It also comes with some bonus features such as fully charging in time for your morning alarm.

With the Life Support charger, you get access to the “Emergency Charge”, “Normal charge”, and “Super-Cautious Healthy Charge” features to help maximize the efficiency of your device's battery.

The Life Support Charger Model 2 comes fully wireless to help you charge your devices with more freedom than ever before!
