Long-lasting battery

Many recent studies show that overcharging the battery when it has already hit 100% deteriorates it. With Life Support, that will be prevented. when the battery of your device hits the maximum healthy charge it stops the charger from accumulating power, and also keeps the battery in a good state resulting in more life and long-lasting battery. Don't worry about unplugging your charger, Life Support does it for you.

How it works

Life Support software implements machine learning to automatically optimize your battery life based on your usage of your device. It also offers the option of customizability for those who know what they want. Through recurrent neural networks, Life Support determines what apps you don’t use, and prevents them from using your battery life. It can also pause wifi and data usage to save battery if deemed battery-draining. Life Support also works while you’re charging. By testing billions of charging scenarios in simulations, Life Support has found the optimal rate of charging for all existing devices and charges based on that. Life Support also continuously updates it’s charging rates with the creation of new devices, that way you never have to worry about a new device.
